帮我写一个可直接运行的 Python 脚本，读取 `cgb_yields.csv`，输入字段为：
- date
- cgb_1y
- cgb_10y

要求：

1. 若输入是日频，按“月末值”转成月频；若已是月频，直接使用
2. 在月频表上计算：
   - `term_spread_10y_1y = cgb_10y - cgb_1y`

### 输出一：无风险利率与长端利率汇总指标

无风险利率主口径：
- `rf_current = latest cgb_1y`
- `rf_1y = mean(last 12 monthly cgb_1y)`
- `rf_3y = mean(last 36 monthly cgb_1y)`
- `rf_5y = mean(last 60 monthly cgb_1y)`
- `rf_10y = mean(last 120 monthly cgb_1y)`
- `rf_20y = mean(last 240 monthly cgb_1y)`

长端利率锚：
- `long_rate_current = latest cgb_10y`
- `long_rate_1y = mean(last 12 monthly cgb_10y)`
- `long_rate_3y = mean(last 36 monthly cgb_10y)`
- `long_rate_5y = mean(last 60 monthly cgb_10y)`
- `long_rate_10y = mean(last 120 monthly cgb_10y)`
- `long_rate_20y = mean(last 240 monthly cgb_10y)`

规则：
- 所有窗口使用简单均值
- 样本不足输出 NaN
- 不计算变化值、分位数、z-score

### 输出二：TERM_SPREAD_10Y_1Y 观察序列

定义：
- `TERM_SPREAD_10Y_1Y = CGB_10Y - CGB_1Y`

用途：
- 用于观察收益率曲线斜率变化

主观察口径：
- 输出 `2024Q4` 到最新的季末值序列

### 数值处理
- 计算过程保留原始精度
- 仅在最终输出时统一 round(2)

### 输出文件
- `cgb_summary_metrics.csv`
- `term_spread_quarterly_view.csv`

其中：
- `cgb_summary_metrics.csv` 包含：
  - `rf_current`
  - `rf_1y`
  - `rf_3y`
  - `rf_5y`
  - `rf_10y`
  - `rf_20y`
  - `long_rate_current`
  - `long_rate_1y`
  - `long_rate_3y`
  - `long_rate_5y`
  - `long_rate_10y`
  - `long_rate_20y`

- `term_spread_quarterly_view.csv` 包含：
  - `date`
  - `quarter_label`
  - `term_spread_10y_1y`
